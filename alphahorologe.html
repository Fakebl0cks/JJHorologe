The History of J.J Horologe!

La Mare De Effendi migrated from France to Indonesia in 1992 with his wife and his newborn wife as of request of his good friend Warkof to oversee his tea factory in Jakarta. On 1995, When Mare contracts are finished, he was offered another contract by Warkof but Mare declined politely as he was eager to seek a better life for his family.

Mare saw an opportunity to sell imported tea to the foreigners and expatriates since tea of other nationality was not fervently imported into Indonesia. Me&Tea, a tea shop of various imported teas was established in Jakarta, Indonesia by La Mare de Effendi to cater for the needs of the expats and foreigners living in late 1995. Maison used his expertise gain from his time working in the tea factory and his previous tea study in France to quickly gain foothold in Jakarta. He imported various high end teas from all over the world to Jakarta and in two years his tea shop was becoming very lucrative due to the amounts of regular customers.

During the 1998 riot in Jakarta, even though his shop was burnt and destroyed to the ground, Mare saw an opportunity to expand quickly as land prices are plummeting everywhere. Six months after the crisis was over, he bought three more property around Jakarta and established more tea shop with Me&Tea brand. With the increase in tea shops, Mare start to stock and serve not only imported teas but also local teas and various local dessert as well as international dessert.

Now in 2016, 24 years after Me&Tea was established, it has 10 branches all over Indonesia and one mega factory and are enjoyed by locals and foreigners. Mare is retiring and Me&Tea will be managed by his only son, Marquis De Effendi.
